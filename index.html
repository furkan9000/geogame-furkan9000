If you click against the traffic flow on a one-way street, the car cannot go backwards. It will calculate a long route looping around the block to reach that point legally.

On big roads with separators (refuges), clicking the wrong lane (opposite side) forces a U-turn at the next exit, wasting huge distance. Zoom in to pick the right lane!
